## Description

Together with a German SME in the area of automotive spare parts we've developed an **API-based application** that takes an image of a spare part, runs a **deep neural network classifier** on it and sends back the predicted part number.

This **speeds up the customer support and ordering process**. Where in the past a qualified employee with years of experience would classify the part, often taking 10 to 15 minutes to identify the exact part number, our AI solution now shows the result in less than a second! On the results page an **image-to-image comparison and result-confidence table** assists the employee in finding the correct part number quickly.

## Advantages

* Increase in employee-productivity
* Decrease in repetitive and mundane tasks
* Based on standardised API (Application Programming Interface): Benefit from the API-economy
* Easy integration with other systems, e.g. websites, mobile apps, backend systems
* Easy integration with business partners
* Collect data and improve the predictions over time
